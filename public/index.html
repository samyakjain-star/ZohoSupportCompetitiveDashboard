ℹ  Scoring Methodology

Rankings are based on a Performance Score computed from two metrics. Each agent is scored against the rest of the team (the agent being scored is excluded from the team average, so results are unbiased).

Ticket Score = (Agent Tickets ÷ Avg Others' Tickets) × 100
Resolution Score = (Avg Others' Res. Time ÷ Agent Res. Time) × 100
Final Score = (60% × Ticket Score) + (40% × Resolution Score)

A score above 100 means the agent is performing above the team average. Tickets are weighted at 60% (productivity) and resolution time at 40% (speed). Resolution score is inverted so that lower resolution time yields a higher score. Agents with no resolution data are ranked on ticket score alone.

Titles are awarded based on score:  Elite ≥150  ·  High Performer ≥120  ·  Proficient ≥100  ·  Developing ≥80  ·  Beginner >0  ·  Inactive =0
